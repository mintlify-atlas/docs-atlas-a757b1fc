---
title: 'Online check-in'
description: 'Complete check-in before arriving at the station to save time at pickup.'
---

The online check-in feature lets you confirm your reservation details from your browser before you arrive at the pickup station. No login is required.

<Note>
  The `/checkin` page is publicly accessible. You do not need to be logged in to use it.
</Note>

## How to check in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to /checkin">
    Open the check-in page at `/checkin` in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your last name">
    Type your last name in the **Apellido** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your IRN reservation code">
    Enter your Individual Reservation Number (IRN) in the **Código de reserva (IRN)** field. This is the unique code from your reservation confirmation email (for example, `ABC123456`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your pickup date">
    Choose your pickup date using the **Fecha de retiro** date picker.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Buscar">
    Click **Buscar** to submit the form. The app connects to the backend to validate your reservation details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Complete check-in up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  Have your driver's license ready to verify your identity when you arrive at the station.
</Tip>

## What is an IRN?

Your IRN (Individual Reservation Number) is the unique code assigned to your reservation at the time of booking. You can find it in your confirmation email or on the `/reservations` page.
