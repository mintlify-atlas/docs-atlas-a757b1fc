Sign in to access your vehicle reservations and account features.

## How to sign in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the sign-in page">
    Open your browser and go to `/login`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your username">
    Type your **Username** into the username field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your password">
    Type your **Password** into the password field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Ingresar** to submit your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You are redirected to the vehicles page">
    On success, your session token is saved and you are taken to `/vehicles`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens on success

When your credentials are accepted:

- The backend returns `{ token: "<jwt>" }`.
- The token is stored in `localStorage` under the key `"token"`.
- You are redirected to `/vehicles`.
- All subsequent API requests automatically include `Authorization: Bearer <token>`.

## What happens on failure

If your credentials are incorrect or the request fails, an error alert is shown. Your stored token is not affected. Try signing in again with the correct credentials.

## API reference

**Endpoint:** `POST /auth/login`

**Request body:**

```json
{
  "username": "your_username",
  "password": "your_password"
}
```

**Success response:**

```json
{
  "token": "<jwt>"
}
```

<Tip>
  After signing in, your token is automatically included in all API requests made through the app. You do not need to attach it manually.
</Tip>
