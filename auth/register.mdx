Create an Underground Fleet account to start making vehicle reservations.

## How to register

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the registration page">
    Open your browser and go to `/register`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your registration details">
    Complete all required fields in the registration form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Submit the form to create your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to access your account">
    After registration, go to `/login` and sign in to obtain your session token and access your reservations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Registration does not sign you in automatically. You must sign in separately after creating your account to receive a session token.
</Note>

## API reference

**Endpoint:** `POST /auth/register`

Submit your registration details in the request body. On success, your account is created and you can sign in immediately.

## Next step

Once your account is created, [sign in](/auth/login) to start using Underground Fleet.
