Create an Underground Fleet account to start making vehicle reservations.

## How to register

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the registration page">
    Open your browser and go to `/register`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your details">
    Complete the three required fields:

    - **Usuario** — your chosen username
    - **Correo electrónico** — your email address
    - **Contraseña** — your password
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Registrarse">
    Submit the form. On success, your account is created, your session token is saved, and you are redirected to `/vehicles`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Registration signs you in automatically. Your JWT token is stored in `localStorage` immediately after a successful registration — you do not need to visit `/login` separately.
</Note>

## API reference

**Endpoint:** `POST /auth/register`

**Request body:**

```json
{
  "username": "jsmith",
  "email": "jsmith@example.com",
  "password": "secret123"
}
```

**Success response:**

```json
{
  "token": "<jwt>"
}
```

On success, the frontend stores the token in `localStorage` and redirects to `/vehicles`.
